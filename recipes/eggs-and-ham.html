Eggs and Ham

A simple yet delicious recipe!

Are you strugging to decide what you whant to have for breakfast? Are your ingredients not enough for a complex recipe? We have something for you! It may not be the most exotic or unique thing you have eaten, but for sure it will help with your hunger.

Maybe you've guessed it already... Yeah, we are talking about eggs! But not just eggs, let's add something else that could help as a flavour twist, what about some ham? We all have it in our fridge, and if that's not the case, we can always go to grocery store and buy a little.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (obviously)
  • Pork ham
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Break the eggs and pour them in a recipient
  2. Add some ham
  3. Add some salt to your taste
  4. Add some oil to your frying pan and let it get somewhat hot
  5. While the pan is getting warm, mix the eggs and ham in your recipient
  6. When the mixture is ready, pour it in the pan
  7. Move the mixture from time to time until it is ready
  8. Finally serve it in a dish enjoy!
