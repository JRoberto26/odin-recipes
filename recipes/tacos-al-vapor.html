Tacos al Vapor

A delicious mexican recipe that feels like home.

This home recipe consists in the well known mexican tortilla filled with various ingredients which you can add to your taste such as chicharrón (a deeply fried beef meat), stewd potatos or beans. You can also put cabbage salad, cheese, onions and/or various sauces if you feel like it!

Ingredients.

  • 1 Kg of mexican tortilla.
  • 500 g of chicharrón
  • 250 g of potatos
  • 250 g of beans
  • 1 cabbage
  • 1 L of vegetable oil
  • Which ever sauce you may like!

Steps.

  1. Make a stew of either potatos, chicharrón or beans
  2. Fill the tortillas with the stews you already prepared and close them
  3. Once all your tortillas are filled and ready put them all in a big cooking pot covered in aluminum and kitchen paper
  4. Pour down all of the hot oil in the tacos, close the aluminum paper and put on the pot's cover
  5. Wait a few hours until the tacos are blend and the oil is completely vaporized
  6. Slice the cabbage and add it over the tacos
  7. Enjoy with your favorite sauce!
