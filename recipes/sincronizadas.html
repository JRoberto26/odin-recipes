Sincronizadas

Enjoy the delicious mexican melted cheese in this easy recipe!

There's a lot of different recipes that come from Mexico that you can enjoy, but nothing is as easy to prepare as quesadillas or even better, sincronizadas!

This recipe consists of the same ingredients and steps as the normal quesadilla with a few twists. For example, you can add whatever you may like to it, maybe some mushrooms, veggies, or why not try a little ham? Let's try with this last one as a firs approach!

Ingredients

  • Mexican tortillas
  • Oaxaca's melting cheese
  • Pork Ham

Steps

  1. Make sure the tortillas are warm, otherwise try giving them a little heat.
  2. Cut some cheese and distribute it along all the tortilla.
  3. Add a slice of ham and close the tortilla.
  4. Cook them for a minute aproximately
  5. Look for the cheese to be melt, if it is then it's ready!
  6. Serve and enjoy!
